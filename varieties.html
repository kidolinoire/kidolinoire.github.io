  • Different Varieties of Potatoes

  • Russet Potatoes

    • Medium to large in size; oblong or oval shaped. Light to medium russet-brown in color, white to pale yellow flesh. Floury texture, light and fluffy when cooked. Mild in flavor, suitable for baking, frying, mashing and roasting.

    Image Credit: georgiafarm2table.com



  • White Potatoes

    • Small to medium in size; round to long shaped. White or tan skin; white flesh. Starchy texture and subtly sweet. Suitable for mashing, boiling and frying.

    Image Credit: weismarket.com



  • Red Potatoes

    • Small to medium sized; round or slightly oblong; smooth, thin red skin; white flesh. Waxy and creamy texture; suitable for roasting, mashing, soups and stews.

    Image Credit: amazon.com



  • Yellow Potatoes

    • Marble to large sized; round or oblong shaped; light tan to golden skin; yellow to golden flesh. waxy and velvety texture; suitable for grilling, roasting and mashing.

    Image Credit: amazon.com



  • Purple/ Blue Potatoes

    • Small to medium in size; oblong to fingerling shaped; deep purple, blue or slightly red skin; blue, purple lavender, pink or white flesh Moist and firm flesh. Earthy flavor; great for roasting, grilling and baking.

    Image Credit:balevbiomarket.com



  • Fingerling Potatoes

    • 2 to 4 inches long; finger-shaped, purple or white skin; red orange, purple, yellow or white flesh. Waxy texture, buttery flavor. Great for pan-frying and roasting.

    Image Credit:stargazerperennialscatalog.com



  • Petite Potatoes

    • Small, bite-sized tiny deliciousness. Great for roasting and frying.

    Image Credit:foodandnutrition.org
